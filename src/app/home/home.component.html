ASPIRE INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL, an initiative of the Sar-la Education Trust, offers new age delivery of education that promotes learning without compromising on inculcating a spirit of competition. The school only delivers an International General Certificate of Secondary Education (IGCSE) syllabus, this is the 1st International School in Nagpur with the IGCSE syllabus. This course is delivered in a student friendly manner with the aim of enhancing understanding and retention. In academic year 2022-2023, the school offers admissions from Nursery to Grade 10 and Grade 11 (Science / Commerce) Maharashtra State Board.

Aspire believes that every student is an individual guided by a unique personality and hence the school offers an education system that weaves academic and artistic excellence with social engagement and moral awareness. In addition to academics, activities aimed at developing the overall personality of the student are offered as part of the curriculum. Various sports and Extra Curricular activities and competitions, such as Art, Music, Dance, Debate and Elocution are planned to promote Public speaking skills and nurture creative expression. The beautiful 9-acre campus in Nagpur offers facilities for various sports including – both Outdoor: Basketball, Football, Throwball, Volleyball, Athletics and Cricket; and Indoor: Badminton, Table tennis and Gymnastics. Aspire School is a student-centered community with a dedicated and caring faculty, small classes and an education that recognizes each student as an individual. Aspire encourages initiative and self-direction in students as they grow and thus creates young adults dedicated to the pursuit of ongoing learning and eager to contribute positively to the global society of the 21st century.

Aspire International School has a mission to prepare children who are lifelong learners with good character values and high academic goal, supported by strong family and community involvement. We give world class education, celebrating the fact that each student is different, as a person and as a learner. We practice learning rather than teaching. We promote understanding of application rather than rote learning of theory. We rear students who are creatively curious and strive to experiment & innovate new things on both academic and non-academic fronts. Our pedagogy is child – centric with emphasis on inquiry and project-based learning. We treasure dynamism and deem that one should be well prepared to adapt to the global scenario and work on his learning accordingly. This is why we have introduced a globally accepted curriculum.
